## AI Strategy Generator

### Daily Strategy

Every morning at 6 AM, our AI generates a personalized trading strategy based on:
- Current market conditions
- Your trading history
- Economic calendar events
- Your preferred strategies

### Strategy Components

Each strategy includes:
- **Market Sentiment**: Bullish, Bearish, Neutral, or Mixed
- **Key Levels**: Support and resistance levels
- **Recommended Pairs**: Best instruments to trade
- **Applicable Strategies**: Which of your strategies work best today
- **Economic Events**: High-impact news to watch

### Customizing Your Strategy

2. Click "Customize"

4. Set your risk tolerance

## Trading Calculators

### Position Size Calculator

Calculate the optimal position size based on:
- Account balance
- Risk percentage per trade
- Stop loss distance
- Instrument

**Formula**: Position Size = (Account Balance × Risk %) ÷ (Stop Loss × Pip Value)

### Risk/Reward Calculator

Analyze your trade setup:
- Entry price
- Stop loss
- Take profit
- Risk/Reward ratio

**Recommendation**: Aim for minimum 1:2 risk/reward ratio

### Pip Value Calculator

Calculate pip value for any instrument:
- Select instrument
- Enter lot size
- Get pip value in your account currency

### Margin Calculator

Calculate required margin:
- Instrument
- Lot size
- Leverage
- Current price

### Compound Calculator

Project your account growth:
- Initial balance
- Monthly return percentage
- Monthly contribution
- Time period

## Checklists

### Pre-Market Checklist

Complete before you start trading:
- [ ] Review economic calendar
- [ ] Analyze daily/4H charts
- [ ] Check overnight movements
- [ ] Review trading plan
- [ ] Assess mental state
- [ ] Set daily risk limits

### Entry Checklist

Verify before entering any trade:
- [ ] Trade aligns with trend
- [ ] Setup meets strategy criteria
- [ ] Risk/Reward is at least 1:2
- [ ] Position size is correct
- [ ] Stop loss is logical
- [ ] No high-impact news soon
- [ ] Emotions are in check

### Post-Market Checklist

Complete after trading:
- [ ] Close all positions (if applicable)
- [ ] Record all trades
- [ ] Review winning trades
- [ ] Review losing trades
- [ ] Calculate daily P&L
- [ ] Update statistics
- [ ] Plan for tomorrow

### Custom Checklists

Create your own checklists:
1. Go to Checklists
2. Click "New Checklist"
3. Add items
4. Set required/optional status
5. Save
